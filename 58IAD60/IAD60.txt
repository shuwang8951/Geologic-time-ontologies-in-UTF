IAD60

IAD60 means "Isotopic Age Determination: 1960 Geologic Time Scale", which has 522 triples. Covers the geological duration from 0 to 600 Ma. Includes 3 Eras (Cenozoic, Mesozoic, Paleozoic), 11 Periods (Quaternary, Tertiary,
Cretaceous, Jurassic, Triassic, Permian, Carboniferous, Devonian, Ordovician, Silurian, Cambrian) and 19 Epoches (Pleistocene, Miocene, Pliocene, Oligocene, Eocene, Paleocene, Maastrichtian, Campanian, Santonian, Coniacian
Turonian, Cenomanian, Albian, Aptian, Neocomian, Bathonian, Bajocian, Visean, Tournaisian) in total. 
Cited from (Kulp, J. L. (1961). Geologic time scale. Science, 133(3459), 1105-1114.)
