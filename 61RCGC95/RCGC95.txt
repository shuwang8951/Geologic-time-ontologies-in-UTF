RCGC95

RCGC95 means "Revised Cenozoic Geochronology-Chronostratigraphy: 1995 Geologic Time Scale", which has 374 triples. Covers the geological duration from 0 to 68.9 Ma. 
Includes 5 Epoches (Miocene, Pliocene, Oligocene, Eocene, Paleocene) and 19 Ages (Lutetian, Bartonian, Langhian, Serravallian, Priabonian, Ypresian, Aquitanian, Burdigalian, Rupelian,
Zanclian, Priabonian, Tortonian, Messinian, Chattian, Piacenzian, Danian, Thanetian, Selandian, Chattian) in total. 
Cited from (Berggren, W. A. ,  Kent, D. V. ,  Iii, C. C. S. , &  Aubry, M. P. . (1995). A revised Cenozoic geochronology and chronostratigraphy. SEPM Spec. Publ.)
