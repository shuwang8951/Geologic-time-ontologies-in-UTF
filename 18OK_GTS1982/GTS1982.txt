GTS1982

GTS1982 means " Phanerozoic Time Scale 1982", which has 1794 triples. Covers the geological duration from 0 to 590 Ma. Includes 199 Geochronologic era in total. 
Cited from (Harland,W.B.,Cox,A.V.,Llewellyn,P.G.,Pickton,C.A.G.,Smith,A.G.&Walters,R.1982.A Geologic Time Scale 1982.Cambridge University Press.)
