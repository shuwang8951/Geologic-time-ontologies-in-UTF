KG1985

KG1985 means " Cretaceous and Jurassic Time Scale 1985", which has 210 triples. Covers the geological duration from 208 to 66.5 Ma. Includes 23 Geochronologic era
 (Hettangian, Singemurian, Pliensbachian, Toarcian, Aalenian, Bajocian, Bathonian, Callovian, Oxfordian, Kimmeridgian, Tithonian, Berriasian, Valanginian, Hauterivian, Barremian
, Aptian, Albian, Cenomanian, Turonian, Coniacian, Santonian, Campanian, Maastrichtian) in total. 
Cited from (Kent D V, Gradstein F M. A Cretaceous and Jurassic geochronology[J]. Geological Society of America Bulletin, 1985, 96(11): 1419-1427.)
