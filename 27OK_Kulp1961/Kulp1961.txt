Kulp1961

Kulp1961 means "The Phanerozoic time-scale 1961", which has 705 triples. Covers the geological duration from 0 to 600 Ma.
Cited from (Kulp J L. Geologic Time Scale: Isotopic age determinations on rocks of known stratigraphic age define an absolute time scale for earth history[J]. Science, 1961, 133(3459): 1105-1114.)
